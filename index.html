Tokenomics

Presale (30%)

3,000,000,000 tokens are allocated for the initial sale to early investors.

Airdrop (10%)

1,000,000,000 tokens will be distributed for users who participate in our airdrop mini game, socialfi, and hold some of our NFTs collections.

Utility (5%)

500,000,000 tokens are designated for utility purposes within the ecosystem, to foster our utilities development.

Farming (5%)

500,000,000 tokens will be used to incentivize users to participate in liquidity farming.

NFT Staking Reward (4%)

400,000,000 tokens are reserved as rewards for staking non-fungible tokens (NFTs). We are looking forward to making NFT staking platforms on our website to boost our NFT use cases.

Team (3%)

300,000,000 tokens are allocated to the project's team members for their commitment in making the project great and successful.

Locked Liquidity Pool (LP) (18%)

1,800,000,000 tokens are reserved to ensure liquidity on exchanges, which is locked for a specified period to build trust and stability.

Burn (10%)

1,000,000,000 tokens will be permanently removed from circulation to reduce supply and potentially increase token value.

Reserve Token (10%)

1,000,000,000 tokens are set aside for future use and CEX listing.

Marketing (5%)

500,000,000 tokens are allocated for promotional activities to increase awareness and adoption like contests, giveaways, and influencer promotion.

Total Supply: 10,000,000,000 tokens
